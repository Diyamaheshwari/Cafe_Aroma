Our Story

Crafted with
Tradition.

Founded in 2024, Café Aroma was born from a simple dream: to bring the tranquil, sun-drenched spirit of Santorini to the heart of the city.

We source our beans directly from sustainable Greek roasters who prioritize quality over quantity. Every cup is brewed using traditional methods, from the classic briki-brewed Greek coffee to our modern, velvety Freddos.

Our commitment extends beyond coffee. We use organic ingredients, locally sourced produce, and time-honored recipes passed down through generations of Mediterranean chefs.
